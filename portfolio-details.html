Wobble Gobble - A Fun and Challenging Puzzle Adventure

*Wobble Gobble* is an isometric puzzle game where players control a jelly-like cube to navigate through challenging levels. The objective is to roll a separate cube onto specific targets to complete puzzles. The game features interactive mechanics like color-matching cubes, buttons, and teleporters, creating a unique and engaging experience. With three distinct worlds and multiple levels, players must think strategically and adapt to the dynamic physics-based gameplay. Available on PC and Android, *Wobble Gobble* offers a fun yet thought-provoking adventure for puzzle enthusiasts.
